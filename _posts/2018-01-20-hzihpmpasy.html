amntenofre:

Temple of the Goddess Hathor at Nitentóre (Dendera),
detail from the west side of the rear wall (upper register, V scene):
the God Schesemu (lion-headed) and the Goddess Ermouthi (Rnnwtt, wearing an Uraeus on Her head) both bringing offering vessels.
To the left, a protective lion protome; below, two guardian lions both crouching upon a shrine, and the two Hapi-Gods of Upper and Lower Egypt performing the Union of the Two Lands (above the symbol of the Union of the Two Lands is represented the sacred phoenix of Ra)
