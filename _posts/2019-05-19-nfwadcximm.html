amntenofre:

hieroglyphs from the “House of Eternity” of Queen Nefertari, QV66, west ‘Uaset’-Thebes:

Ḥ(w)t-Ḥr(w) ḥryt-tp W3st nbt
pt ḥnwt Nṯrw nbw

whose meaning is

“Hathor the chieftainess of ‘Uaset’-Thebes, the Lady
of the Sky, the Queen of all the Gods”
