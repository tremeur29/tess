Belted Galloway, Yorkshire Dales, England

by Hill Top Farmgirl

I remember I would pass a field of these majestys on the train to college, and they always confused me as if never saw a cow like this before and they were all like it. Eventually I googled “striped cow” and found that they’re Belted Galloways. My now second favourite type of cow, after Highland of course.
