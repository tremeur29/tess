James Stirling, Queen’s College, St. Clements, Oxford, England, 1966-1971

Living in this building was not the best thing in the world, when the only cooking facility was a single microwave shared between 75 people, the stair rails gave off static shocks, each shower had its own problem, the underfloor heating was uncontrollable, some of the loos had no sinks, a number of windows leaked and numerous tiles had fallen off the walls.
